OAT COOKIES

Time: 30 minutes  |  Calories: 87 cal  |  Serving: 20  |  Other: Easy

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Bananas300 g
Oats200 g
Nuts50 g
Chocolate Drops50 g

Note: Nuts can be shredded nuts or replaced with nut butter.

Macros

Carbs: 13 g  //  Protein: 2 g  //  Fat: 3 g

Directions

  1. Step I
    Mash 2–3 bananas with a fork. Add in 200 g oats, 50 g shredded nuts (or nut butter), and 50 g chocolate drops. Mix everything together.
  2. Step II
    Using a teaspoon, shape small cookies and place them on a baking tray. Bake for 15 minutes at 160°C.
