CHICKPEA CURRY

Time: 20 minutes  |  Calories: 623 cal  |  Serving: 2  |  Other: Vegan

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
(Wholegrain) Rice160 g
Canned Chickpeas200 g
Chopped Tomatoes200 g
Coconut Milk200 ml
Coconut Oil5 g
Green Beans200 g
Onion1
Garlic Cloves1
Curry1.5 tsp
Garam Masala1 tsp
Bunch of Cilantro1

Directions

  1. Step I: Prepare the (wholegrain) rice as usual in a rice cooker or boiling water.
  2. Step II: Heat coconut oil in a large pan. Add finely chopped garlic, ginger and onions and sauté for about 2–3 minutes.
  3. Step III: Wash the green beans, cut off the ends and halve them. Add them into a pan with a few sips of water and let simmer for another 5 minutes.
  4. Step IV: Add the chopped tomatoes and coconut milk. Let everything boil briefly and then add the drained chickpeas. Let everything simmer for another 10 minutes.
  5. Step V: Add the spices (curry, garam masala, salt and pepper) and mix well. Serve with rice and cilantro.

Macros

Carbs: 78 g  //  Protein: 17 g  //  Fat: 27 g

Garam Masala (Optional Homemade Blend)

Garam Masala is a blend of ground seasonings. You can buy it as a mixture or make your own.

  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1½ tsp ground coriander
  • 1½ tsp ground cardamom
  • 1½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ tsp ground cloves
  • ½ tsp ground nutmeg
