MEXICAN BOWL

Time: 30 minutes  |  Calories: 620 cal  |  Serving: 2  |  Other: Meal Prep

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Rice160 g
Black or Kidney Beans80 g
Red Bell Pepper1
Sweet Potato300 g
Minced Beef200 g
Red Onions1
Garlic Cloves1
Bunch of Cilantro1
Lime1
OptionalDip (from page 65)

Directions

  1. Step I: Prepare the rice with 1.5× the water either in a rice cooker or in a regular pot. Preheat the oven to 180°C. Peel one of the red onions, halve it and cut into rings. Chop the other onion into small cubes as well as the garlic clove.
  2. Step II: Wash and cut the bell pepper into small stripes. Peel and cut the sweet potato into small cubes or stripes. Add the bell pepper, onion rings and sweet potato cubes all separate on an oven tray. Top with 20 g olive oil, salt, pepper and chili powder.
  3. Step III: Bake in the oven for 25 minutes. In the meantime, heat some olive oil in a pan. Add minced beef and roast for 3 minutes. Add chopped onion and garlic to the beef. Once the beef is done, remove from the pan and season with salt, pepper and chili powder.
  4. Step IV: Rinse the beans. Chop the cilantro. Prepare the dip of your choice (from page 57). Grab a big bowl, fill with rice and mix with salt, chopped cilantro and lime juice. Add the beef, the oven veggies, beans and top with your favorite dip.

Optional: Dip from page 65.
