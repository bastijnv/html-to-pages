ESPRESSO BANANA BREAD

INGREDIENTS

Flour Mix — 200g
(100g Flour / 100g Oat Flour)

Banana — 300g
Coffee — 150ml
Coconut Oil — 50g
Cinnamon — 1/2 tsp
Baking Powder — 2 tsp
Nuts — 50g
Chocolate drops — 50g

MACROS

Carbs — 30g
Protein — 6g
Fat — 14g

Time — 60 minutes
Calories — 270 cal
Serving — 2
Other — Vegan

DIRECTIONS

STEP I

If you use oat flour, add 100g oats into a food processor and mix until you get a floury consistency. Mix regular flour & oat flour in a big bowl, add 2 tsp baking powder and 1/2 tsp cinnamon.

STEP II

Mix 50g fluid coconut oil together with 150ml black coffee. Add the bananas in a big bowl and mash until smooth. Mix the coffee into the banana mash.

STEP III

Mix the liquid banana mass with the flour and let the batter stand for 5 minutes. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees in the meantime.

STEP IV

Fold in 50g chopped nuts & chocolate drops into the dough. Add into a loaf pan and bake for 45–50 minutes. You will get roughly 7 big slices of banana bread.
