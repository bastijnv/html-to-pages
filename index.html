SWEET POTATO BROWNIES

Time: 60 minutes  |  Calories: 100 cal  |  Serving: 12  |  Other: High Carb

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Sweet Potato300 g
Oatmeal (Oats)120 g
Apple Pulp150 g
Cocoa50 g
Chocolate Drops70 g
Baking Powder1 tsp

Directions

  1. Step I: Peel 300g sweet potatoes and cut into small pieces.
  2. Step II: Cook the sweet potato in hot water until soft for about 20–25 minutes.
  3. Step III: Add 120g oats into a food processor until you get oatmeal and put aside.
  4. Step IV: Mix the oatmeal with 1 tablespoon baking powder and 50g cocoa.
  5. Step V: Preheat the oven to 180°C. Blend the sweet potato in a food processor. Mix the sweet potato mash, 150g apple pulp and 50g chocolate drops.
  6. Step VI: Mix the mash with the dry mass until you get a smooth dough. Add chocolate drops on top and put the dough in an oven tray.
  7. Step VII: Bake for 45 minutes. Let the brownies cool down before cutting into pieces.

Notes

The brownies are very chocolaty. If you like more sweetness, you can add 30–50g honey or agave syrup.
