HIGH PROTEIN BROWNIE

Time: 30 minutes  |  Calories: 210 cal  |  Serving: 6  |  Other: High Protein

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
(Oat) Flour50 g
Ground Almonds30 g
(Choco) Protein Powder30 g
Baking Powder10 g
Cocoa40 g
Milk (any)120 ml
Eggs2
Banana100 g
Chocolate chips60 g
Optional: Nuts on Top(optional)

Macros

Carbs: 17 g  //  Protein: 13 g  //  Fat: 10 g

Directions

  1. Step I
    Prepare the flour by adding both oats as well as almonds (or use ground almonds) in a mixer. Add into a bowl and mix the flour with baking powder, cocoa and protein powder.
  2. Step II
    In a separate bowl mix milk and eggs. Smash the banana and add to the mixture. Mix well until you get a smooth consistency.
  3. Step III
    Now mix the fluid with dry ingredients until you get a smooth batter. Put aside for 5 minutes. Then fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Step IV
    Add the dough into a baking pan and bake for 20 minutes at 180°C. Let the brownies cool down for a few minutes before cutting them into 6 big pieces.
